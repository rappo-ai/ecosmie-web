About Us

ECOSMIE INDIA is a software company based in Bengaluru, India.

There has been an explosion in the use of messaging platforms like WhatsApp, Messenger, etc. in recent years. These messaging platforms are increasingly being used for business. As a result there is a need for automation tools to manage business workflows on these platforms.

ECOSMIE is a boutique software company that specializes in building chatbots for automation of business process on messaging platforms. We build and deploy bots around Support, FAQ and e-commerce use cases.
